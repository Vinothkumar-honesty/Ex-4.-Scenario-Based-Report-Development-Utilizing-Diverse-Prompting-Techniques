# Ex-4.-Scenario-Based-Report-Development-Utilizing-Diverse-Prompting-Techniques
Objective: The goal of this experiment is to design and develop an AI-powered chatbot that can handle customer inquiries, provide support, and improve customer experience in a retail environment. Create prompts using various AI prompting techniques to guide your experiment, data collection, analysis, and report creation.
## Aim: 

To design and develop an AI-powered chatbot capable of handling customer inquiries, providing support, and enhancing customer experience in a retail environment through effective prompt engineering and evaluation.

## Algorithm: 

1. Define Scenario: Establish a consistent retail use case, such as an inquiry about a delayed order, to use as a baseline for all tests.

2. Select Patterns: Identify the seven specific prompting techniques to be tested: Zero-Shot, Few-Shot, CoT, Instruction, Role, Structured, and Unstructured.

3. Execute Baseline Tests: Run Zero-Shot and Unstructured prompts to observe the AI's performance without specific guidance or context.

4. Apply Contextual Patterns: Use Few-Shot and Role-Based prompts to prime the model with examples and a specific professional persona.

5. Implement Logical Frameworks: Deploy Chain-of-Thought (CoT) and Structured prompts to force step-by-step reasoning and organized data handling.

6. Run Formatted Instructions: Apply Instruction-Based prompts to enforce strict rules regarding politeness, product details, and support sign-offs.

7. Collect Raw Data: Capture and document the unique chatbot response generated by each of the seven individual prompting methods.

8. Evaluate Performance: Score each output against five key metrics: Accuracy, Clarity, Relevance, Creativity, and Customer Friendliness.

9. Analyze Comparative Results: Organize the scores into a tabular format to identify the strengths and weaknesses of each specific approach.

10. Determine Optimal Strategy: Select the highest-performing pattern and finalize a report with recommendations for retail chatbot implementation.

## Prompt:
### A. Zero-Shot Prompting 
```
Answer a customer asking about their delayed order for UltraGear Headphones.
```

### B. Few-Shot Prompting

``` (After providing 2 examples of successful resolutions) "Now, respond to a customer with a delayed headphone order.```


### C. Chain-of-Thought (CoT) Prompting
```
Analyze the customer issue step-by-step and then provide the best solution for a delayed headphone order.
```

### D. Instruction-Based Prompting

```
Respond politely, provide product details (UltraGear: Noise cancelling, 40hr battery), and end with a support message.
```

### E. Role-Based Prompting


```
You are a smart AI shopping assistant helping customers. A customer is upset about a delayed order.
```

### F. Structured Prompting

```

[Query: Delay] [Product: UltraGear] [Tone: Empathetic] [Constraint: No specific dates]
```

### G. Unstructured Prompting

```
Help with late headphones.
```

## Output:
### A. Zero-Shot Prompting

Response: "Your order for UltraGear Headphones is currently delayed. We apologize for the inconvenience. We are working to ship it as soon as possible. Please check your email for tracking updates."

### B. Few-Shot Prompting

Response: "I understand it's frustrating to wait for your UltraGear Headphones. I’ve checked the status, and it’s currently in the final sorting hub. As a gesture of goodwill for the delay, I’ve applied a 10% discount code [RETAIL10] to your account for your next purchase. You should receive a tracking link within 24 hours."

### C. Chain-of-Thought (CoT) Prompting

Response:

Acknowledge: The customer is frustrated due to a late delivery of a premium item.

Verify: Check logistics (item is backordered).

Action: Offer an immediate update and a compensation option.

Draft: "I have analyzed your order. Due to high demand, the UltraGear Headphones are on a 3-day backorder. I will prioritize your shipment the moment stock arrives and have credited ₹500 to your loyalty wallet for the wait."

### D. Instruction-Based Prompting

Response: "Hello! We are sorry for the delay of your UltraGear Headphones—the ones with industry-leading noise cancellation and 40-hour battery life. We are ensuring your unit passes all quality checks before shipping. If you have further questions, please reply to this chat or call our 24/7 support line."

### E. Role-Based Prompting

Response: "Hi there! I’m your AI assistant, and I truly regret the delay with your UltraGear Headphones. I know you’re excited to try out that wireless freedom! I'm personally monitoring your order's progress to ensure it gets to you quickly. Is there anything else I can help you find while we wait?"

### F. Structured Prompting


Response: "We sincerely value your patience regarding your UltraGear Headphones. While we cannot provide a specific delivery date at this moment due to logistical shifts, we guarantee that your order is our top priority. We will notify you via SMS the moment it leaves our warehouse."

### G. Unstructured Prompting

Response: "Sorry the headphones are late. We will ship them soon."


## Result:

The AI-powered chatbot was successfully designed and tested using different prompt engineering techniques to handle retail customer queries effectively. It improved response accuracy, clarity, and customer support quality, especially when using structured and context-based prompts.
